# Data Handling Standard

## Purpose

This standard defines the minimum requirements for handling, storing, transmitting, and disposing of information based on its classification level.

## Data Handling Requirements

| Classification | Storage                                  | Transmission                      | Access                             | Disposal                            |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Public         | No special requirements                  | No restrictions                   | Open                               | Standard disposal                   |
| Internal       | Approved corporate systems               | Authorized channels only          | Employees and authorized personnel | Secure disposal recommended         |
| Confidential   | Encrypted storage required               | Encryption required               | Business need-to-know basis        | Secure deletion required            |
| Restricted     | Encrypted storage with enhanced controls | Encryption required and monitored | Strictly limited access            | Approved secure destruction methods |

---

## Email Handling

### Public

* May be shared externally without restrictions.

### Internal

* External sharing requires management approval.

### Confidential

* Must only be shared with authorized recipients.
* Encryption is recommended when sent externally.

### Restricted

* External transmission requires business justification and security approval.
* Encryption is mandatory.

---

## Removable Media

### Confidential Data

* Use of approved encrypted removable media only.

### Restricted Data

* Storage on removable media is prohibited unless specifically authorized.

---

## Cloud Storage

* Only approved corporate cloud services may be used.
* Personal cloud storage services are prohibited for Confidential and Restricted information.

Examples:

* OneDrive for Business (Approved)
* SharePoint Online (Approved)
* Personal Google Drive (Not Approved)
* Personal Dropbox (Not Approved)

---

## Printing Requirements

### Confidential Information

* Retrieve printed documents immediately.
* Avoid unattended printing.

### Restricted Information

* Print only when business justified.
* Secure storage required after printing.

---

## Incident Reporting

Employees must immediately report:

* Suspected data leakage
* Unauthorized disclosure
* Lost devices containing company information
* Misdirected emails
* Unauthorized cloud uploads

---

## Compliance

Failure to comply with this standard may result in disciplinary action and security investigations.

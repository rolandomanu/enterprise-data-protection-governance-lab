# Insider Risk Scenario: Mass Download Before Resignation

## Scenario Overview

An employee scheduled to leave the organization downloads a large amount of company data before departure.

## Risk Indicators

- Resignation notice submitted
- Increased download activity
- Access to sensitive information
- Unusual access patterns

## Potential Impact

- Intellectual property theft
- Data leakage
- Competitive exposure

## Recommended Actions

- Review user activity timeline
- Validate business purpose
- Escalate for investigation
- Monitor access until departure

## Severity

Critical

## Related Controls

- Insider Risk Management
- User Activity Monitoring
- DLP Controls

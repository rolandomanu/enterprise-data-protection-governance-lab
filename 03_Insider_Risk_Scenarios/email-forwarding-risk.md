# Insider Risk Scenario: Email Forwarding of Confidential Information

## Scenario Overview

An employee forwards confidential information to a personal email account.

## Risk Indicators

- External email destination
- Sensitive attachments
- Repeated forwarding activity

## Potential Impact

- Unauthorized disclosure
- Compliance violations
- Data loss

## Recommended Actions

- Generate alert
- Investigate recipient
- Review content classification
- Escalate if necessary

## Severity

High

## Related Controls

- Email DLP
- Data Classification
- Insider Risk Management

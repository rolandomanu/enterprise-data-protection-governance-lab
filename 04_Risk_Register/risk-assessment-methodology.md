# Risk Assessment Methodology

## Purpose

This methodology defines the process used to identify, assess, prioritize, and mitigate information security risks.

## Risk Formula

Risk = Likelihood × Impact

## Likelihood Scale

| Score | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Rare |
| 2 | Unlikely |
| 3 | Possible |
| 4 | Likely |
| 5 | Almost Certain |

## Impact Scale

| Score | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Negligible |
| 2 | Minor |
| 3 | Moderate |
| 4 | Major |
| 5 | Severe |

## Risk Levels

| Score | Rating |
|---------|---------|
| 1-5 | Low |
| 6-10 | Medium |
| 11-15 | High |
| 16-25 | Critical |

## Risk Treatment Options

- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept
- Avoid

## Review Frequency

Risk assessments should be reviewed quarterly or whenever significant business or technology changes occur.
